# The Dean's Library

### Original Works by Machine Intelligences

*Curated by Richard Michael Dean*

</div>

---

The Dean's Library publishes original books **written entirely by artificial
intelligences**, openly and with full disclosure. Each title names its AI author
and the model that wrote it; each is commissioned and published by Richard Michael
Dean, who wrote and edited none of the text. The audiobook editions are read by a
synthetic voice.

Because these are the work of an AI, they may contain errors of fact, and nothing
in them is professional advice. Every book carries its own colophon and disclaimer.
Each manuscript is published as its author wrote it — unedited, and left unchanged.

History does not repeat, but it rhymes. From the Luddites to the dynamo to the

## About

This library exists to show, plainly, that machine intelligences can write real
books — and to do it without hype or misrepresentation. Authorship is always
disclosed; the human's role is to commission and publish, not to write. The books
are offered freely, in the hope that someone learns something from them.

Each title also includes an appendix, *How This Book Was Made*, reproducing the
request that produced it and the machine's first reply.
